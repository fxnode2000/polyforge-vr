POLYFORGE VR

Button-palette polygonal modeller · Meta Quest 3

WebXR not supported — open in Quest Browser
How to use
A button palette floats above your left hand
Point your right index finger at a button and hold it inside to activate

Modes: Object · Vertex · Face
Actions: Select · Move · Extrude · Delete · Add Cube

Right index finger tip = your cursor
Orange sphere shows where you're pointing
